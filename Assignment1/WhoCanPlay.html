Who can Play

Volleybal is a safe, joyable sport that does not require extreme fitness. Since you don’t have to move around the court too fast, it is great for people who want a low-impact sport that will help them get fit without pushing them to their limit.

Also, volleyball can be played by those who are a bit more competitive, as this sport can be really exciting and intense. But the player must bare in mind that there is always a net between them and their opponents.

Beach volleyball is suitable for people of all ages, both men and women alike.
Anyone considering to start a new sport or exercise program should see a doctor first.
